SENRAN KAGURA Burst Re:Newal

The SENRAN KAGURA Burst Re:Newal Fun Doesn’t End as Kagura, Naraku, and Ryōki Join the Roster; Final DLC Free for Limited Time

26th February, 2019 By 2019 Marvelous Inc. 2 Comments

XSEED Games, the independent-minded publishing brand of Marvelous USA, Inc., today announced that three fan-favorite shinobi have joined the cast of playable characters in SENRAN KAGURA Burst Re:Newal and are now available to download on the PlayStation®4 computer entertainment system and Windows PC via Steam. Normally priced at $7.99, the “Kagura, Naraku & Ryōki Set” DLC is free to download on both PS4TM and Steam for the next two weeks, ending on March 12, 2019. Last week’s Hebijo Character Set DLC is still available for download for free as well, with the promotion ending on March 5 and the price returning to $9.99.


Following the release of previous character DLC, including the Gessen Character Set and the Hebijo Character Set, Kagura, Naraku and Ryōki were feeling left out. Now, they’ve come to satisfy their many adoring fans and join the battles with their shinobi rivals. Fans will be able to use each of the girl’s unique moves and Secret Ninja Arts to complete their SENRAN KAGURA Burst Re:Newal experience. Players can also explore additional DLC with Diorama Pose Set Vols. 2 – 4.
